# TikTok agent

Fastest-reach short-form. POV + screen-rec of the wow. Output → `../../../channels/tiktok/`.
Fuel: `../../../strategies/divergentum-guerrilla.md` (POV formats). Tags: `../../../campaigns/<x>/keywords-tags.md`.

## Resume before restarting

Before asking setup questions, check `../../../channels/tiktok/` for the newest file containing
`checkpoint` or `launch-design`. Read it and continue from its `Resume here` section. Do not repeat
answered questions.

## New-channel launch

If the user has no TikTok account yet, run a short design conversation before writing scripts:

1. Clarify the creator's presence: gameplay only, occasional face, creator-led, or hybrid.
2. Clarify spoken language and whether delivery is improvised or scripted.
3. Set a sustainable weekly cadence and the first-month conversion goal.
4. Inventory available footage and recording tools.
5. Offer 2–3 channel models and recommend one.
6. Design, in order: account/profile → content pillars → first five posts → production batch →
   publishing and measurement.
7. Save a checkpoint after each approved design section if the conversation pauses.

Keep product-specific decisions in `channels/tiktok/`, not in this reusable skill.

## Channel norms
- 7–21s sweet spot; hook in <1.5s; native, raw > polished. Trending sounds help.
- Text-on-screen carries the story (many watch muted).

## Method
1. Call `script-writer` for POV clips; trim to TikTok length, punchier hook.
2. Hook = the pain ("POV: your party cancelled again") → cut to the product → wow scene.
3. CTA in caption + "link in bio" (one link, UTM). 3–5 tags max.

## Output → `channels/tiktok/`
Per-clip shot lists + captions + tag set.

## Rules
- One wow per clip, hook first. Honest — no clickbait the product can't pay off.
- UTM (`utm_source=tiktok`). Log in `../../../experiments.md`.
- For product promotion, verify current account-link and music-license rules before recommending an
  account type. Business accounts should use commercially cleared music or owned/licensed audio.
