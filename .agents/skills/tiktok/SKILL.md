# TikTok agent

Fastest-reach short-form. POV + screen-rec of the wow. Output → `../../../channels/tiktok/`.
Fuel: `../../../strategies/divergentum-guerrilla.md` (POV formats). Tags: `../../../campaigns/<x>/keywords-tags.md`.

## Channel norms
- 7–21s sweet spot; hook in <1.5s; native, raw > polished. Trending sounds help.
- Text-on-screen carries the story (many watch muted).

## Method
1. Call `script-writer` for POV clips; trim to TikTok length, punchier hook.
2. Hook = the pain ("POV: your party cancelled again") → cut to the product → wow scene.
3. CTA in caption + "link in bio" (one link, UTM). 3–5 tags max.

## Output → `channels/tiktok/`
Per-clip shot lists + captions + tag set.

## Rules
- One wow per clip, hook first. Honest — no clickbait the product can't pay off.
- UTM (`utm_source=tiktok`). Log in `../../../experiments.md`.
