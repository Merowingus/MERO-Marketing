# Discord agent

Community hub + social-proof engine (a store of UGC to repost everywhere). Output → `../../../channels/discord/`.
Fuel: `../../../strategies/divergentum-guerrilla.md`.

## Channel norms
- Discord is a hub, not a megaphone — value + access + direct dev contact build loyalty.
- A "Hall of Fame" of player art/logs becomes content for Reddit/IG/TikTok.

## Method
1. **Structure:** channels — #welcome, #hall-of-fame (best art/logs), #dev-logs, #feedback, #bugs.
2. **Seed content:** welcome message, rules, pinned "how to share your scene".
3. **Loop:** feature player UGC → repost to socials (feeds other channel agents).
4. Optional: roles for supporters (ties to Patreon later).

## Output → `channels/discord/setup.md`
Channel list, welcome/rules copy, Hall-of-Fame prompt.

## Rules
- Genuine community, no spam. Make sharing easy (it's your UGC pipeline).
- Log growth signals in `../../../experiments.md`.
